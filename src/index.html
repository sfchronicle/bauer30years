30 years of restaurant innovation

Michael Bauer’s timeline of Bay Area dining history

By Michael Bauer

September 2016

This is a caption. Person Person, The Chronicle

It’s not often we take time to look back, reflect and see what brought us to our present day. On my 30th anniversary at The Chronicle, I’ve decided to do just that. I’ve compiled a year-by-year list of the restaurants that moved the dining scene forward, picking the most important restaurant from each of my 30 years. It’s not always the best restaurant of the year — or even the most prominent — but ones that offered something special and made the Bay Area the food epicenter it is today.
— Michael Bauer
